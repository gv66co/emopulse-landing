Problem

The gap between body data and emotional truth

Current solutions measure the body but fail to understand emotions. Wearables track heart rate, mood apps track check-ins – but neither captures real, moment-to-moment emotional clarity.

  • Market gap: tools that see numbers, but not feelings.
  • Customers: individuals & teams needing real-time emotional insight for better decisions, communication, and well-being.
  • Usability: existing tools are fragmented, complex, and rarely fit into real life.

Solution

Real-time emotional insight, from a single camera

Emopulse closes the gap: we blend pulse analytics, micro-signals, and AI into one clear emotional insight – delivered instantly from your device camera.

  • One-tap experience: look at the camera, get emotional clarity.
  • Sensor-free: no wearables, no extra hardware, no friction.
  • Scalable SaaS: API-ready, enterprise-ready, and built for modern teams.
